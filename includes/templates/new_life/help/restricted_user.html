Basic Overview

This tool is intended as a way for you to easily interact with other users and administrators, collaborating on client projects. By uploading files and interacting with users on a per client basis, you can streamline your workflow and get more accomplished collectively.



Clients

Manage My Clients

Administrators have given you access to the clients listed in the "Manage My Clients" area. There are three major functions of this software : Notes, Users, and Files.

To view or add notes to a client, choose "Notes". To view all users associated with a client and quickly send an email, choose "Users". To view or download files associated with a client and to attach a file you have already uploaded to the client, choose "Files".

User Access

You are given access to a client on a per-client basis. When the administrator granted access for a client, you received an email listing the date you will no longer be able access the client. After that date, the client will no longer show up in your "Manage My Clients" area.



Files

Uploading

Choose the "Add" button when viewing the your "Manage My Files" area. Simply enter a title, some notes, and choose the file to upload and the rest is handled by the system. You can easily sort by file type, size, and owner in both the "Manage My Files" section.

Access

You have been given permission to upload files to the system. Do not abuse it. You can only edit files which you have uploaded.

You cannot delete any file.



Notes

Creating and Editing

Notes are company specific. Choose "Notes" from the "Manage My Companies" area to view all notes. Choose "Add" to enter a new note and share with other users.

User Access

A user can view all notes for a client, but can only edit notes he has posted. A user can never delete a note.
