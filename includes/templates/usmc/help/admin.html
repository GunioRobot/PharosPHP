Basic Overview


This tool provides you with access to manage the content that users see on the facebook Application, as well as track your content popularity.



Dashboard


Content

The dashboard provides you with quick access to important information, such as the top most viewed features, shows, or videos.

iPhone

There is an iPhone Web Application you can use to view the Dashboard from your iPhone. Visit the Dashboard on iPhone at:
{HTTP_SERVER}iphone/.



Media Library

The Media Library is your one stop for managing content. Whether adding a new feature, plug, show, or video, always use the "+ Add" tab to create a new item and then fill out the appropriate information. Searching and sorting of the tables help you find your content quickly as your collection grows.

Preview Application

Your changes are recorded while adding and editing content. However, before your users will see the updated content, you must click the "Publish Application" button to make your changes active.

In order for you to view your changes before publishing the application, choose "Preview Application" to view a live version.


Publish Application

Your changes are recorded while adding and editing content. However, before your users will see the updated content, you must click the "Publish Application" button to make your changes active. Once published, your changes will become live on the facebook application.




Accounts

Add new administrators and edit existing user information.
